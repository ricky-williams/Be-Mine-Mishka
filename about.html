About Us

Construction Galaxy (CG), is a Black Owned private company by Koketso Moremi. An entrepreneur residing in the south of Johannesburg with an aim to help uplift the economically disadvantaged community, making a difference to the economy simultaneously and try by all means to combat socio-economic issues like poverty in society.

Using our combined experience, technical expertise, and local knowledge, we are able to deliver projects and services that contribute to the community, our economy and future generations.
